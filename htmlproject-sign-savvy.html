Tech-Savviest star signs

Find your sign and see how tech-savvy you are!!

 
Star sign Tech Savvyness
Aries This sign is motivated by taking control of a task and will put a lot of energy into it, so people will be impressed by the focus and concentration that elicit from Aries. Probably most suited for frontend work, as appearance and the need to have something to show others, is very important.
Taurus Serious and grounded, will take one step at a time to complete any task. Well suited for task that are realistic and not too flimsical or 'out there'. Very patient and will complete projects, not the fastest but most thorough.
Gemini This sign needs colleagues and is a connaisseur when it comes to memory having many ideas. It's important to have a team leader that decides which way to go in a project. Best suited for IT support, fixing a broad scope of small tasks.
Cancer This sensitive water sign needs to feel supported in work. Imagination will come in handy for designing websites and Cancer will have the best feeling of what the end user needs in web apps.
Leo Here is a leader. Not necessarily in real life, but in his own kingdom. This playful sign will try to make his worth show at work, and will need to have a say in projects. Best suited for broad tasks that he can put his name on, like an artist.
Virgo The most intelligent of the signs is also the one with most attention to detail. Here is a solid employee that will serve a company a lifetime maybe, and she will not leave any project unattended for long. Best suited as manager or for testing projects for errors/bugs.
Libra The scales of the zodiac is good when it comes to compromise, so here is one that levels crazy ideas and will take the mean road to completion. Best suited for aestethic projects that also has some beauty to show.
Scorpio This sign is probably the deepest looking of all. A true backend programmer. Doesn't care for frontend as long as it binds up to a whole. Best suited for cybersecurity and ethical hacking
Sagittarius Freedom loving and jovial. A fun colleague that can talk his way out of anything. Well suited for fullstack dev, espsially remotely, but maybe more playful og meaningful apps will be best for him, like quality games with some philosophic og historic relevance.
Capricorn The serious sign with great timing in humour. backend programming is great for her, but may aspire to let the public get to know her somehow. As a career-oriented sign, she may advance to company leader some day.
Aquarius Does it all with a natural reassuring and authentic no-bullshit intelligence. As a cool excentric he knows how good he is, and will do his best to always be better. Like Mozart he can take something advanced and make it simple for others. Best suited for fullstack dev.
Pisces Imaginative and sensitive. Best suited for remote freelance work, as the waves of uncertainty and misguidance is always near. Will feel trapped in a 9-5 job. Best suited for graphical frontend work or maybe cybersecurity.
